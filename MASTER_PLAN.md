## THE STRATEGY: THREE PILLARS

### PILLAR 1: THE DEAL ROOM (Your Unfair Advantage)

Every horse you represent gets a dedicated, beautiful "Deal Room" page. This is NOT a classified listing. This is what a $500K listing presentation looks like in luxury real estate, applied to horses.

**Each Deal Room contains:**

1. **Hero Video** — 60-90 second cinematic video. Opens with the money shot (sliding stop, jumping, trail riding beauty shot). Buyers watch 7 seconds before deciding — make them count
2. **Photo Gallery** — Professional conformation shots (both sides, front, rear), action shots, barn/lifestyle photos. 10-15 minimum
3. **Pedigree Chart** — Interactive family tree going back 3-4 generations. Color-coded for champions, producers, money earners
4. **Performance Record** — Show results, earnings, LTE (lifetime earnings), NRHA/NCHA/AQHA points, race records
5. **Vet & Health Section** — PPE summary, x-ray availability, soundness history, vaccination records. Transparency = trust
6. **Training History** — Who trained this horse, what level, what discipline crossover potential
7. **Video Library** — Multiple riding videos (different riders, different settings), ground manners, loading, farrier
8. **Price & Terms** — Clear asking price or price range. Financing options if applicable. Trial period policy
9. **Inquiry Button** — One click to express interest. Captures name, email, phone, budget, discipline, timeline
10. **Broker's Take** — YOUR personal written assessment. "Why I chose to represent this horse." This is the trust builder

**Why this wins:** When you send a buyer a link to a Deal Room vs. some Facebook post with 3 blurry photos, you've already won. The professionalism gap is massive. Buyers will SHARE these links with their trainers, spouses, and barn friends — free distribution.

## SMS STRATEGY (The Secret Weapon)

SMS has a 98% open rate and 90-second average response time. For time-sensitive horse sales, this is critical.

### When to Text (Not Email):
1. **New listing alert**: "New: 2022 AQHA gelding, reining prospect, $35K. Photos & video: [Deal Room link]. Reply INTERESTED"
2. **Price drop**: "[Horse name] now $38K (was $45K). Motivated seller. Reply or call to schedule"
3. **Show presence**: "We're at NRHA Futurity this week with 3 horses available. Want to schedule a ride?"
4. **Follow-up after inquiry**: Text 24 hours after email inquiry — "Hi [Name], saw your inquiry about [Horse]. Happy to answer questions or set up a video call"
5. **Appointment reminders**: "Your trial ride with [Horse] is tomorrow at 10am. Need directions? [link]"

### Compliance (Non-Negotiable):
- TCPA: Must have explicit written opt-in for marketing texts
- 10DLC registration required for business texting
- Every text must include opt-out ("Reply STOP to unsubscribe")
- Limit to 2-4 marketing texts per month
- Let subscribers choose channel preference at signup (email only, SMS only, or both)
